How to Cite

If you use the Raṣāʾif corpus in research, teaching, or any published work, please cite it as follows.

APA (7th edition)

Al-Ghamdi, A. H. S. (2024). Raṣāʾif: An Arabic–English parallel corpus of classical scholarly and literary texts [Online corpus]. Retrieved from https://ahmedhsalghamdi.github.io/arabic-english-rasaif-corpus/

MLA (9th edition)

Al-Ghamdi, Ahmed H. S. Raṣāʾif: An Arabic–English Parallel Corpus of Classical Scholarly and Literary Texts. 2024, ahmedhsalghamdi.github.io/arabic-english-rasaif-corpus/.

Chicago (17th edition)

Al-Ghamdi, Ahmed H. S. Raṣāʾif: An Arabic–English Parallel Corpus of Classical Scholarly and Literary Texts. Online corpus, 2024. https://ahmedhsalghamdi.github.io/arabic-english-rasaif-corpus/.

BibTeX

@misc{alghamdi2024rasaif, author = {Al-Ghamdi, Ahmed H. S.}, title = {{Raṣāʾif}: An {Arabic}--{English} Parallel Corpus of Classical Scholarly and Literary Texts}, year = {2024}, url = {https://ahmedhsalghamdi.github.io/arabic-english-rasaif-corpus/}, note = {Online corpus} }

Citing individual texts

When citing a specific text within the corpus, also cite the original edition and translation used. Provenance details for each text are available on the About page.
