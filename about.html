About the Corpus

Raṣāʾif (رصائف, lit. paved ways or causeways) is a parallel text repository of classical Arabic scholarly and literary works alongside their published English translations. The corpus is designed for researchers in Arabic linguistics, translation studies, and computational humanities.

Scope and categories

Texts are organised into two primary categories:

  • Scholarly Works — including jurisprudence and legal theory (fiqh), medicine and natural sciences, biographies and ṭabaqāt literature, and general scholarship.
  • Literary Works — including classical artistic prose (nathr fannī) and the maqāmāt genre.

Further categories may be added in future versions of the corpus.

Alignment

Texts are aligned at the sentence or paragraph level. Each Arabic unit is paired with its corresponding English translation unit. The alignment is manual: every pair has been verified against the source and translation texts to ensure correspondence is genuine and not merely positional.

The gold dot (●) visible in parallel table view marks each aligned pair, indicating that the Arabic and English in that row are genuine translation equivalents.

Technical notes

  • The corpus is entirely client-side: all text data is served as static files and all search, lemmatisation, and statistical computation runs in the browser.
  • Arabic morphological matching uses a rule-based light stemmer that strips common prefixes and suffixes to approximate roots. It is not a full morphological analyser.
  • English lemmatisation uses a rule-based suffix-stripping approach with a small irregular verb table.
  • PMI (pointwise mutual information) is computed over a sliding window of ±4 tokens, after stopword removal.

Adding new texts

To add a text to the corpus, create two plain-text files (Arabic and English) with one sentence or paragraph per line, add an entry to corpus/index.json under the appropriate category and subcategory, and deploy. The corpus structure is designed to accept new files without modifying any application code.

أبواب الذخيرة وأقسامها

تنقسم النصوص إلى بابين رئيسيين:

  • الرصائف العلمية — وتشتمل على الفقه والأصول، والطب والعلوم الطبيعية، والتراجم والطبقات، وسائر العلوم.
  • الرصائف الأدبية — وتشتمل على النثر الفني، والمقامات.

ويُرتقب إضافة أبواب أخرى في الإصدارات القادمة.
