How to add a new text to the corpus

    

      
1

      
Drop your .docx or .csv file above. It must have two columns — Arabic and English.

    

    

      
2

      
The tool auto-detects which column is Arabic. Confirm or swap if needed.

    

    

      
3

      
Fill in the text's metadata (title, author, date, source). Choose the right category.

    

    

      
4

      
Click Convert & Download Files. Two files download: yourtext_ar.txt and yourtext_en.txt.

    

    

      
5

      
On GitHub, upload both .txt files into the correct folder under corpus/ — e.g. corpus/scholarly/medicine/.

    

    

      
6

      
Open corpus/index.json on GitHub. Click the pencil ✏️ to edit. Paste the JSON block shown above into the correct texts array. Commit.

    

    

      
7

      
Done — the text is live on the site within a minute.
